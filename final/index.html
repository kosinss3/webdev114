Fresh. Sustainable. Food.

The goal of the business is to provide sustainable healthy food. This will be done both in a lunch bistro style restaurant that serves fresh meals made from scratch in-house every day, and a greenhouse where guests can buy living herbs and vegetable plants. The main greenhouse will also provide a space that can function as a venue for events such as farmers markets or dinner party events.
