Our mission:

Our goal is to create a sustainable business and food supply for our local community. We accomplish this by not only serving great healthy food in our restaurant, but also by being a space that the community can gather for events such as farmers markets.

What we offer:

Our food is made fresh from scratch in-house every day. The focus of our food is health minded and sustainably sourced. The menu takes inspiration from a variety of cuisines, but is heavily influenced by South East Asian cuisine.

Have a garden, or just like to keep herbs on your windowsill? Visit our greenhouse and browse our selection of vegetables, herbs, and other house plants.

If you're looking for a show stopping venue for your next event you can book an even to be hosted in our garden greenhouse.
